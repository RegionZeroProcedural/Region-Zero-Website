# Region Zero

![Region Zero Banner](resources/banner.png)

## About

Region Zero is a solo-developed zombie survival game focused on procedural exploration, experimental weaponry, unique perks, and endless replayability.

Fight through a classified military research facility overrun by infected horrors. Unlock new regions, discover powerful upgrades, and survive increasingly dangerous waves while uncovering the secrets buried deep beneath the facility.

---

## Features

* Procedurally Generated Maps
* Endless Zombie Survival Gameplay
* Experimental Weapons
* Regional Progression System
* Unique Perks
* Dynamic Enemy Encounters
* Community-Driven Development
* Active Development Updates

---

## Current Regions

### Cafeteria

The starting area where survivors first enter the facility.

### Freezer

A frozen storage sector containing rare perks and dangerous enemies.

### Viral Research

The heart of the outbreak where experimental pathogens were developed.

### Region Zero

The deepest and most dangerous part of the facility.
